Their Story

A love 25 years in the making

It started around February 2002. Steve will tell you he chased Jan for twenty-some years — and that she finally gave in. The truth, of course, is that some things are simply meant to be.

They set February 22nd as the date that started it all. Now, exactly twenty-five years later, they’re getting married on that very same day — because some dates are just written in the stars.

Most people assumed they were married already. And as Steve likes to point out, by common law they’ve probably been married three times over. But now they’re making it official — not because they need to, but because a love this enduring deserves to be celebrated.
